ABOUT TTC

We are a student led group that sponsors and leads outdoor activities. Our student trip leaders lead trips on a weekly basis with stuff like hiking, camping, climbing, caving, etc. We are open to all registered students, faculty and alumni of the University of Maryland. All levels are welcome from novice to expert.

Mission Statement: To bring together students and alumni in outdoor activities to appreciate, learn, and have fun, all within the natural environment. To provide training and outdoor related skills at all levels.

WHEN/WHERE DO WE MEET?

Time:Every Monday at 7pm

Place: 0131 Armory

Purpose: To inform members for future trips, have pre-trip meetings for upcoming trips, buy TTC swag, pay dues/sign waivers, and just to socialize. It's TTC tradition to go to Jason's Deli after the meeting.
